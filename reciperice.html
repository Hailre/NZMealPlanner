Egg Fried Rice

INGREDIENTS
1 Tbsp rice bran or vegetable oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 cups (400g) cooked rice
3 eggs, beaten
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp oyster sauce
1 tsp sesame oil
½ cup frozen vegetables of your choice
15g butter
Finely sliced spring onion, if desired
